Our Youth

We believe all youth can embrace who they are, can define their future, and can change the world.

Our Vision

Our vision
at E & F Sports is to create a thriving community where every child, regardless of their socio-economic background, has equal opportunities to succeed. We envision a future where immigrant and low-income youth in South King County are empowered through sports and education, unlocking their full potential and becoming leaders in their communities. By fostering an inclusive and supportive environment, we aim to inspire a generation of resilient individuals who embrace diversity, contribute positively to society, and make lasting change. Through our efforts, we envision a brighter and more equitable future for all.

Our Mission

Our mission at E & F Sports is to empower immigrant and low-income youth in South King County through our community basketball league. We are committed to providing a safe and engaging after-school program that promotes academic achievement, leadership development, and community engagement. By fostering a love for sports and education, we aim to inspire and equip the next generation of confident, compassionate leaders who will make a positive impact in their communities. Together, we strive to create a brighter future for all children, regardless of their background or circumstances.
